Who We Are

A Community of Faith and Profession

The Fellowship of Christian Nurses (FCN), Obangede Chapter, is a student-led body dedicated to the spiritual and professional growth of nursing students at the Kogi State College of Nursing Sciences. Our mission is to build a strong community of Christian nurses who are not only clinically competent but are also spiritually grounded, ready to serve as witnesses for Christ in the healthcare field.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of God's love and healing in the healthcare community of Nigeria and beyond.

Our History

Founded by passionate students, our chapter has grown into a vibrant fellowship supporting hundreds of future nurses.
